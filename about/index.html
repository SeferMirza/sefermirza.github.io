About

The company, originally named İkinoktabir Yazılım San ve Tic Ltd Şti, was established in 2014 by Cihan to provide his professional services independently. In 2019, Gülnur joined the company as a partner, and İkinoktabir became a legal entity dedicated to serving the separate professional services of both Gülnur and Cihan.

Each whole transcends its parts and deserves its own name.

After three years of partnership, Gülnur and Cihan realized that their company deserved a unique identity. In 2022, they decided to rebrand as GC Brains, a name to represent the experience, skill and vision of its partners.

GC Brains, with the people or corporates it serves, establishes a long-term ground with mutual trust that makes it possible to work with love and ownership, and on this ground it does better together.
